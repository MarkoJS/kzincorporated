-KZ incorporated-

Since March 2010, KZ Incorporated has been on a mission to deliver new levels of service for the USA logistic industry, for our drivers, and our partners.

Part of CHI Group

Smooth transport of goods is a great basis for business performance. Our portfolio ranges from a comprehensive parcel service and US-wide general cargo solutions to challenging partial and full loads. This gives you major advantages for the development and daily coverage of the US markets.

KZ Incorporated was established in 2010. We have over 12 years of experience in Freight Forwarding and Logistics solutions and apply that to today’s ever-changing rules and regulations ensuring your cargo is never held up.
Our staff has many years of experience in fields across the logistics, local freight, international freight, and IT industries.

Our people come from all continents and all cultures, but what they have in common and what makes the team special is that they share a passion for trucks and transportation. We employ and promote people who bring energy and creativity every day.

We pride ourselves on being a family-owned and operated business, ensuring our clients receive the personalized service required for a successful business partnership.

Our results and the satisfaction of drivers and customers, improve the way the company operates. To succeed in this we have defined our vision and mission that leads all those who are part of the company across the world.
